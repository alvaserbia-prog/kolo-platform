# Rulebook on Founding Contribution

*Version 4.0.0*

*Amendment 4.0.0 (22.07.2026): consolidation of the entire documentation set to version 4.0 upon the entry of the KOLO Foundation in the Register of Endowments and Foundations (registration number 28836627, PIB 115840443); version references updated. Unchanged in substance.*

*This Rulebook governs the operational mechanics of founding contribution — the retrospective recording of work performed before the platform was opened. It is adopted on the basis of Article 37 of the KOLO System Rulebook.*

*Amendment 3.9.1 (Art. 6, 7, 8, 12, 14): the step amount is changed from 20,000 to 24,000 POEN and the total number of steps from 120 to 100; the last step is activated at the threshold of 10,000,000 POEN, after which the channel closes permanently. The upper limit of 2,400,000 POEN remains unchanged.*

## I — General Provisions

### Article 1

*Subject matter*

This Rulebook governs: the concept of a founder and founding work, the upper limit of founding contribution, the amount and schedule of recording steps, the condition for activating a step, the point at which the channel closes permanently, and the distribution of founding contribution among founders.

Terms not defined in this Rulebook shall have the meaning established in the KOLO System Rulebook.

### Article 2

*Relationship with the KOLO System Rulebook*

This Rulebook elaborates the provisions of Article 37 of the KOLO System Rulebook. In the event of any inconsistency, the provisions of the KOLO System Rulebook shall prevail.

## II — Founders and Founding Work

### Article 3

*Concept of a founder within the meaning of this Rulebook*

A founder is a natural person who performed work on the design, development, and preparation of the KOLO system before the platform was opened.

The circle of founders is closed. The persons acquiring founder status are established by an internal act of the Foundation published in accordance with Article 13 of this Rulebook. No decision adopted after the publication of that act may expand the circle of founders.

### Article 4

*Subject matter of founding work*

Founding work encompasses work performed before the platform was opened, in particular: the design of the system architecture, the drafting of rules and protocols, legal preparation, organisational preparation, and the preparation of system documentation.

Founding work is by its nature operational contribution performed before the system existed and therefore could not be recorded at the time it was carried out.

## III — Upper Limit and Recording Steps

### Article 5

*Upper limit*

The upper limit of founding contribution is 2,400,000 POEN. This is the total maximum amount that the Protocol may record through the founding contribution channel.

### Article 6

*Recording step*

Founding contribution is recorded in steps of a fixed amount of 24,000 POEN. Each step records the same amount, without exception.

The total number of steps is 100.

### Article 7

*Condition for activating a step*

The Protocol records one step each time the total number of POEN recorded in the system reaches a new threshold of 100,000 POEN.

The thresholds are fixed: 100,000, 200,000, 300,000, and so on, up to and including the final threshold of 10,000,000 POEN. The Protocol activates a step at the moment the total number of POEN in the system first reaches or exceeds the next threshold. After the step activated at the threshold of 10,000,000 POEN, founding contribution is no longer recorded.

POEN recorded through founding contribution are included in the total number of POEN recorded in the system and count towards the cumulative growth that activates the next step.

### Article 8

*Effect of a step on the accounting coefficient*

The fixed-amount step is designed so that its relative impact on the accounting coefficient decreases as the system grows. In the early phase of the system, when the total number of POEN is small, a step of 24,000 POEN constitutes a larger share of the total. As the system grows, the same absolute amount represents an ever-smaller share — so the greatest part of the impact of founding contribution falls in the early phase, before ZRNO is activated, while the accounting coefficient does not yet play an operational role.

## IV — Recording Mechanics

### Article 9

*Method of recording*

The recording of founding contribution is an automatic act of the Protocol. When the total number of POEN in the system reaches the next threshold, the Protocol records the step without any discretionary decision by any actor.

### Article 10

*Founders' records*

POEN recorded through founding contribution are entered in the founders' records and have the same status as all other POEN — records in the ledger without a property right of the user, non-convertible and without external value.

### Article 11

*Relationship with other recording channels*

Founding contribution is a separate recording channel and does not affect the operational contribution limit.

A founder who registers ZRNO is subject to the same threshold and the same limit per accounting period as any other user.

## V — Distribution Among Founders

### Article 12

*Internal distribution*

The distribution of the upper limit of founding contribution among founders is established by an internal act of the Foundation.

The internal act establishes the share of each founder in the total upper limit. The sum of all shares equals the upper limit set out in Article 5 of this Rulebook.

Each step of 24,000 POEN is distributed among founders in proportion to their shares established by the internal act.

### Article 13

*Publication of the distribution*

The internal act on the distribution of founding contribution is published to system users.

## VI — Closure of the Channel

### Article 14

*Point of closure*

The founding contribution channel closes permanently at the moment the Protocol records the 100th step — that is, when the total amount recorded through founding contribution reaches the upper limit of 2,400,000 POEN.

### Article 15

*Permanence of closure*

The closure of the founding contribution channel is permanent and irrevocable. The channel may not be reopened by a decision of the founders, the Foundation, the Management Board (UO), or the Upper Kolo.

## VII — Transparency

### Article 16

*Public availability of data on founding contribution*

The following data are publicly available to all system users: the total amount recorded through founding contribution, the number of steps completed, the remaining amount up to the upper limit, the percentage of channel utilisation, and the founders' distribution shares.

## VIII — Final Provisions

### Article 17

*Amendments to the Rulebook*

This Rulebook is adopted and amended by the Management Board (UO) of the KOLO Foundation, under the procedure established in the KOLO System Rulebook.

### Article 18

*Entry into force*

This Rulebook enters into force on the date of its adoption by the Management Board (UO) of the KOLO Foundation.
